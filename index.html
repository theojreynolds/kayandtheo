Kay & Theo’s
1 Year Anniversary

Hi Kay! It’s been exactly one year (that’s 365 days) since we met. Isn’t that absolutely mental? I made a little something for us to look back on our journey together. I really hope you like it. Happy anniversary darling!

The early months

2 September

We met at a cute little coffee shop. You said hi to me first. And you fell in love with my weird (cute?) pick up line.

During our first couple of weeks together, we hung out a lot. I took you to very random places. All of them wintry, because you had mentioned that winter was your favourite season. After a while we got bored of the snow and the cold, but never of each other.

1 week

You did a very bold thing within our first week of dating. You told me you loved me. This could be completely false, but I think I was leaving for tennis or some other thing, when you told me to take care and then said those three words. I didn’t respond to it, because I hadn’t felt that way about you yet.

12 September

This was the night you first told me about your heart condition. I’d never wanted to envelop someone in a hug as badly as I did in that moment with you. You said that you’d understand if I wanted to leave you now. But I said no, I’d stay.

2 weeks

I couldn’t leave you hanging on those words for much longer. I remember we were having a heartfelt conversation, I don’t know about what, possibly about your heart. But at the end of it you told me again that you loved me. And that I didn’t have to say it back. But I wanted to prove you wrong. So I said it back. And I meant it.

18 September

Our first song! You shared it with me for the first time, saying it reminded you of me. I thought to myself how much I loved you. I played it 24 times in a row for the next couple of days after.

26 September

The day I found out that you wrote your own songs. Remember how I couldn’t even form a coherent sentence for a good few minutes? I was mindblown, to say the least.

2 October

Our 1 month anniversary. And… I proposed to you. You’d been talking about getting married for a while, and I felt it was the right moment to do it. Aren’t you happy I did it?

9 October

This was our first big argument. You told me about your past relationship. I didn’t take it well and didn’t speak to you until the morning after. I was upset that you hid it from me. And I was envious that I wasn’t your first. But you gave me the reassurances that I needed, like you always manage to do.

28 October

This is one of my favourite quotes from you. I had been unwell for a week and was annoyed that I’d messed up my sleep, but you said this to console me and I felt so relieved I could have cried.

2 November

Our 2 month. We were supposed to get married on this day, but we had an argument, I forget about what. I was irritated and not in the mood for a wedding anymore, so we called it off.

3 November

We got married! We resolved whatever issue it was from the day before and went ahead to the little forest place. You looked like a fairy with your flower crown, long wavy hair, and white dress. I stood there admiring you for a while, and then we said our vows.

13 November

We got married, again! This time at the beach and you had a different, more extravagant dress, which I was obsessed with.

21 November

I suppose this was when our relationship really started showing its cracks. You had fainted on the way to school the day before, and I didn’t hear from you for more than 36 hours. When you did eventually message me, I was so angry and didn’t talk to you that day. You didn’t know why, but I think even though I was so relieved you were okay, I was also angry at you for putting me through that fear. It was not your fault, of course.

28 November

This is when we ended. I couldn’t trust you, and you said there is no relationship without trust, so I broke up with you. I was angry. We had been patchy at best throughout the previous week, little irritation here and there, at least on my part. And I reached a breaking point. I will always regret what I did. Because I hurt you, and because of what happened afterwards.

The dark days

2 December

Damn this is hard. I’m reading our conversation from this day, when we were really speaking for the first time after the break up. I was showing you the letters I had written in my Google document. We were cold towards each other, which was understandable. But by the end of it you said this, and I wanted to cry.

10 December

By this point I already got the feeling that you had taken to someone else. I knew that you had been spending a lot of time with other people. Obviously, since we weren’t together anymore. But I texted you that evening and you said this. Again I wanted to cry.

14 December

The day before, I had told you that I wouldn’t be able to talk to you on this day because I was meeting with some friends. That was true. But then we didn’t talk at all for 6 days. You thought that I was upset with you, but I was just giving you some space … to let you move on from me. I don’t know if you knew, but I would check on you every day. If I saw you were online I’d know that you were okay.

24 December

Christmas Eve. Do you remember what happened on this day? I do, and remembering about it now is bringing me to tears because of how scared I felt, how terrified I was of losing you. I knew you were scared too, so I stayed with you, even though you insisted I be with my family.

26 December

Let’s not talk about what happened. If you forget, you can just read the letter that I wrote this day.

29 December

We saw each other’s faces for the first time. I love how your hair shines in the sunlight. Absolutely beautiful.

1 January

Let’s not talk about what happened (part 2). It was easily the worst start I could have had to the year. I woke up to your messages panicking that I was upset with you, when I didn’t even know what had happened yet. When you told me though, unlike 26 December, it didn’t feel like a punch to the stomach or like my heart had been ripped out. Because I had seen it coming. And I dreaded it and hoped it wouldn’t come.

The next week after that was hell.

The kiss and make up

14 January

I kissed you! For the previous week we had been getting really close again with each other, spending a lot of time together. One day you fainted at school and had to stay at the hospital. You messaged me saying you would really like some cuddles, but that you’d given up hope of me kissing you back. At the time I actually was scared to do it, because I knew what would eventually happen if we kissed. I didn’t want to hurt you and mess us up again. But, my wants were stronger than my fears, so I kissed you. And apparently even until today, that was the hottest kiss I’ve ever given you.

16 January

You showed me this song and asked me out, again. And I said yes, again. Second best thing I’ve ever done?

19 January

We got married for the third time. And you took a picture of it too!

22 January

I think this was our first date after getting back together. You started taking a lot of pictures, not to post, but to save for ourselves. I’m grateful to you for doing that because now we have so many memories that we can look back on.

29 January

Our second date. And the first time we matched in a long while! I do actually really love this picture.

5 February

Our third date, on the beach. You wore your elegant shimmery dress and I was in my … comfy kitten pyjama.

13 February

Valentine’s date. Since Valentine’s was on a school day, we decided to celebrate it early. You took us to a very cute place to take pictures. I was obsessed with your hair and red skirt. I was even more obsessed when we got home though, because you put on your very first scandalous outfit. And later that night, you said to me, “I wanna make love to you.” Immense butterflies.

21 February

No date, but you showed me this gorgeous white dress. You are just so pretty.

26 March

Matching date! I always love it when I match with you. This was a fun day, and you took a lot of pictures of us. Just look at you showing off your wedding ring.

2 April

Our 7 month. We only took one picture, with you in a swimsuit. You know how I love you in a swimsuit.

12 April

This was our first big argument after getting back together. We argued about three things, and got angry and irritated with each other. Notice how this is almost three months after we got back together? I remember thinking at the time, is this our limit? Because last time, when we almost reached three months, we fought too, and that ended in our split. I never wanted or thought about splitting up with you again, I was just scared of the possibility of that happening. Fortunately, after nearly 14 hours of talking, we managed to work things out.

14 April

A couple of days after that, we went on a photoshoot. You took some of my most favourite pictures of us. My crush on you grows a little bigger every time I see you in black.

29 May

Who am I kidding, my crush on you grows a little bigger every time I see you full stop. We took so many silly pictures on this day. I always have so much fun with you. Oh, and your hairstyle here is one of my favourite ever.

21 June

This was to be our last date for a while. I remember it as a bright, cheerful day. You didn’t take many pictures, but I do love this one that you took of yourself.

22 June

Our last normal day. We talked for hours and hours. It was such a fun time.

The storm clouds

23 June

Everything changed for us after this day. My world kind of went topsy-turvy. And of course it affected you too.

The next week or so would be the worst period of my life. There was so much chaos and turmoil inside me already. Added with the fact that there was a real possibility of losing you. It was awful.

2 July

Our 10 month. You gave me the most wonderful anniversary gift ever. I didn’t think we would be celebrating this day together, but we did, because you came back. I was so shocked, but so happy.

From then on though, we would go through a rough time. We’d get to see each other way less often and less freely than we used to. We’d argue quite a bit as well, because we had to adjust and figure out how to spend the little time we have together as best as we could.

14 August

My birthday. I was delighted to get to spend my birthday with you. We got to hang out for a while, you became our photographer once again, and it felt a little like the old times.

23 August

Your birthday. I was so excited to celebrate your birthday with you. Unfortunately we only got to enjoy the day for five minutes before we were stopped.

29 August

You know when I said it’s been 365 days since we met, I lied. It has only been 361 days. I’m showing you all of this early, since we don’t know if we can even talk on our anniversary.

Anniversary. This will be the first time we’re using that word correctly. We’ve been saying 1 month anniversary, 5 month anniversary. But now we’re so close to reaching the real anniversary. Our 1 year.

We’ve both gone through so much this past year. Our relationship hasn’t been smooth sailing, at all. There was a time when we thought we would never see each other again. There were times when we would argue, and in our anger, we would only see the hurt, forgetting about our affection for each other. But we would always work it out. And we would grow stronger by the end of it.

I made this because I wanted to celebrate our relationship. But I also want to celebrate you. You deserve so much credit for being patient with me. I know I can be difficult to put up with, especially when I’m in one of my irritable moods, as I have often been these couple of months. Always, you show me endless love and kindness.

Every day I think about how lucky I am to have you. And I can’t help but pray the same blessing for everyone out there.

Thank you, Kay. Thank you for allowing me into your life. Thank you for the so many laughter-filled days you’ve given me. Thank you for standing by my side when times are hard. Lastly, thank you for the best year of my life.

Here’s to many more years of us. I love you always.

— Your Theodore 💛
